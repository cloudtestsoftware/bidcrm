Placing Sample Order

Below are steps to start a sample order

Step -1: Select Item and add Quantity (2-5 max)

  • Select any item in the table
  • Enter the quantity you need
  • Click on the Add To Cart checkbox
  • Add multiple items, if you need more items sample

Step -2: Fill up Order Delivery Form on the right hand side

Enter your name and shipping address. If your company has FedEx account or you want us to use your delivery method, you can provide this info in the FedEx account input box. This is optional.

Step -3: Place your order

  • Make sure you added all items you need
  • Also check your delivery form if any thing need to be changed
  • Click the place order buuton finally
  • You will receive an email confirmation after your order got placed successfully!
